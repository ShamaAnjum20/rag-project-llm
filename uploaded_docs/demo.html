<h1>rag document</h1>

<p>RAG, or Retrieval-Augmented Generation, is an AI framework that enhances Large Language Models (LLMs) by connecting them to external data sources (like documents, databases, or the web) to provide more accurate, up-to-date, and contextually relevant answers, acting like an "open-book" exam for the AI instead of relying solely on its internal training data. It works in two stages: retrieval (finding relevant info from external knowledge) and generation (using that info to form an informed answer), reducing "hallucinations" and increasing trustworthiness by grounding responses in verifiable facts.
